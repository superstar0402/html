Frequently asked questions

Q: How long does the course last
A: The lectures are within a week. The dev project can take a few days or a few weeks, depending on ambitions and knowledge level.

Q: Can I pay by Paypal / credit card / something else?
A: Unfortunately, only the methods listed in the pricing section are possible.

Q: Why do I need to pay an extra 21% (VAT)?
A: This is just how traditional finances work when selling services to individuals - there isn't anything we can do about it.

Q: What is the included dev project like?
A: Up to you, really! A few ZK languages will be introduced, their pros and cons explained, some possible project ideas presented and then it's up to you. No rating or reviews will be done for the project.

Q: Why not just offer this course on Youtube/Udemy?
A: The course material is not the most important part here. Results are much better with real human interaction and discussions.
