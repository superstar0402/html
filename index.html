Zero Knowledge Intro Lecture

This lecture is designed for anyone interested in understanding the basics of Zero Knowledge technology. The lecture is interactive and free for anyone.

What you'll learn

After the lecture, you should:
  • Understand what is ZK
  • Understand some of the use cases
  • Be part of a supportive ZK community (Discord)

Next available lectures

  • 12th of December at 18:00 WET (1h)
  • 14th of December at 16:00 WET (1h)
  • 19th of December at 16:00 WET (1h)

Each lecture will accept only five participants. Every lecture has the same contents. A lecture starts by introductions, followed by some slides and ends with discussions.

Prerequisites

You have to:
  • Understand and be able to speak English well
  • Be able to participate in the interactive lecture: have a decent microphone and be in a quiet spot
  • Be civil

There are no explicit knowledge requirements for the course. However, it helps if you understand blockchains at least a bit.

Signup

To join any of the lectures, please fill in the signup form below. Do note:

  • Participants are chosen based on the order they sign up and on their answers in the signup form.
  • If you are approved, you will be emailed with an invitation to the Google Meet lecture. If you do not accept the invitation within two days, the spot may be given to someone else.
  • Please respect my time by showing up to the lecture if you are accepted.
